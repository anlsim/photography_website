About Andrea Simental

Andrea was born and raised in Mexico where she attended high school and college. She was interested in the visual arts but it was until she moved to Seattle in 2014 that she discovered her passion for photography.
The wide variety of landscapes that the Pacific Northwest has to offer captivated her desire to learn and be a part of the world of photography.
She takes every opportunity when she travels to practice. She often focuses her attention on taking photos about nature and long exposure photos, especially at night, star trails and astrophotography are her favorites! After volunteering for two years as a photographer in ST. Johns Pre-School she realized how fun a photo shoot can be. Recently she started taking photo shoots for families and couples.
Andrea currently attends to Lake Washington Institute of Technology where she studies Digital Design and plans to graduate in the summer of 2018.
